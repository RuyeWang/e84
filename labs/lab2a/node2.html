Transistor Testing

Different types of transistors are used in the AM/FM radio receiver kit of this lab, including five 2N3904 transistors (also available in the stock room). Use the Type 575 semiconductor curve tracer in the electronic lab to test these transistors in common emitter mode. Observe and take pictures of both the input and output characteristic plots.

Important note: To make sure you do not burn your transistor, (a) study the datasheet of the transistor to determine the leads for E, B and C, (b) do not apply voltage higher than 30 V.
