Instruction (read this before you start)

Instructions

  • Take-home, open-notes, open-internet, allow yourself no more than 4 hours (can be more than one sitting), specify the start and finish times on the first page of your submission.

  • Write your name on top of each page. Also label each page by the page number and total number of pages, e.g., 3/5 for the third of all 5 pages in total.

  • When solving a problem, list the main steps. In each step describe concisely what you are doing, then show the calculation and the result of the step. Box all final answers.

  • A final answer, even if correct, without evidence of the steps leading to the answer will receive no credit.
