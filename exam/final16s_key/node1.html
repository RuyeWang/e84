Instruction (read this before you start)

  • Take home, open everything except discussion. Due 12 noon, May 11, in my office.
  • Mark your start and end times. Don't spend more than 4 hours.
  • Compare your print-out of the exam with the online version to make sure your hard copy is complete.
  • Mark your name and question number clearly on top of each page. Indicate the total number of pages submitted.
  • You should be able to solve all problems by hand. If you feel you have to rely on some software tools (calculator, Matlab, or any other ``solver''), you may be doing something wrong.
  • When solving a problem, list all the steps. In each step, concisely state what you are doing in English, then show the calculation and the result of the step.
  • A final answer, even if correct, without evidence of the steps leading to the answer will not receive credit.
  • Box all answers.
  • Don't click the ``next'' link for the problems until you are ready to take the exam.
